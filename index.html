All of my designs incorporate a strong foundation of Composition and Color Theory. As a keen Photographer, I aim to make everything I create something that is visually immersive and illustrative whilst providing functionality and ease of use.
I use WordPress to create custom themes for clients in order to help their website run smoother. Websites often need to be maintained and regularly updated which can be costly and inefficient. Once the sit is built, WordPress provides an easy and simple way for the client to update their details themselves without breaking the site or losing any time.
Responsive Design is a huge necessity for any website. In 2018, 52.2% of all website traffic worldwide was generated through mobile phones and it will only increase more as we progress in our digital age. With this in mind I build my sites for mobiles first to ensure the best possible user experience on all devices. Adding animation to a website is a simple and very powerful tool that can increase the user experience exponentially. Simple slides and fades will immediately give you site a professional look and feel that is unrivalled. Graphic Design is essential for any business that wishes to devlop their online presence further. The look and feel of any website can often be greatly determined by the right juxtaposition of colours or the correct use of negative space.
