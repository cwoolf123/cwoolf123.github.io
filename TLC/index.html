Services

We have have over 20 years experience in supporting and helping businesses grow so you can be assured we will work with you to achieve the best possible service in a friendly and reliable manner.

Accounting Services

  • Medium-sized business
  • Individuals looking for personal tax planning and advice
  • Business start ups.

Book Keeping

At TLC we can assemble your paperwork which will be used to prepare your accounts, VAT returns or produce management accounts.

Payrole Services

  • Preparation of payslips for staff
  • RTI Submissions to HMRC
  • Setting up Auto-Enrolment Pension schemes to comply with The Pensions Regulator

Construction Industry Scheme

  • Verifying subcontractors
  • Producing CIS statements of payments and deductions
  • Dealing with CIS suffered offset for Limited Companies.

VAT

  • Submission of quarterly VAT returns
  • Calculations based on cash accounting and traditional methods
  • Flat rate scheme including review of the new 'limited cost business'

Taxation Services

  • Self Assessment - Individuals who are required to complete Self Assessment Tax Returns such as rentals.
  • Limited Companies - We specialise in catering for owner manager limited companies.
